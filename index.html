Plumbrowse - Plumbing Opensearch bookmarks for your browser

Try These:

  • Drag one of the links to your bookmarks bar.
  • Copy any text from any application. Or if you're on Linux, just select anything.
  • Click the bookmark. The search page will open, with your clipboard content as the query

How It Works

Bookmark a link like
http://localhost:45678/opensearch?url=http://example.com/search?q={searchTerms}.
The url parameter is an Opensearch URL Template.

Click the bookmark, and this server will put the clipboard's contents into {searchTerms} and redirect to the resulting link.
